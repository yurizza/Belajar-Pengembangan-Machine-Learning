Classification BBC News Demo

This demo illustrates the capabilities of a pre-trained model to categorize BBC News articles into SPORTS, BUSINESS, POLITICS, TECHNOLOGY, or ENTERTAINMENT. The model excels in predicting the specific category of a news article based on its content. The training process is executed server-side using Python and then seamlessly converted into a TensorFlow.js model for usage in the browser.

The model is trained using truncated BBC News content, restricted to a maximum of 100 words, and utilizing only the top 50,000 most frequent words in the dataset.
